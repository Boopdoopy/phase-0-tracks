Day 0

The S.S. Queen Disney sank today. There were 8 others who joined my fiance and I on this little life raft. 6 other passengers and the ship's greeter.

We turned to cannibalism within the first hour. Neither of us had eaten since breakfast and my fairer half is an excellent leader in a crisis.

Not that we were hungry, mind you. But curing meat properly takes days and as Tony Robbins always says: The path to success is to take massive, determined action.
